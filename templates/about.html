Who we are

We are a family from Bulgaria that has been rescuing homeless animals for years. Discarded, newborn, maimed, run over... The number never drops below 20+, so our costs are huge. Until recently, we managed with personal funds, but in the last two years, it has been unaffordable, especially after 3 consecutive cases of cats with FIP in the past year (2021). In order to be able to continue helping, we have started making wrought iron products, the sale of which will support the animals we take in. Beds, dog and cat feeders, cat shaped planters, wall hangers, as time goes on I hope our range will expand. Everything is handmade, powder coated, wrought iron. Please take a look, and if you fancy something, you can order on Viber or Whatsapp- 0898868694 (still only there)
